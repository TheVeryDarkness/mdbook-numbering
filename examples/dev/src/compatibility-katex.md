## Compatibility with KaTeX

This section demonstrates that the numbering plugin works correctly with the [katex] preprocessor.

$$
\begin{align}
              E & = mc^2 \\
    \mathcal{L} & = \frac{1}{2} m v^2 - V(x) \\
\end{align}
$$

```latex
\begin{align}
              E & = mc^2 \\
    \mathcal{L} & = \frac{1}{2} m v^2 - V(x) \\
\end{align}
```

[katex]: https://crates.io/crates/mdbook-katex/0.10.0-alpha
